How to use the links:

Click the Copy Link button next to your required build. Once copied, open a new browser tab or launch your phone's primary internet browser (like Chrome or Safari), paste the link into the address bar, and go!

Why do this? Sometimes heavy server downloads can freeze, hang, or glitch if launched directly inside social media built-in apps or in-app web views. Pasting directly into an external browser fixes this entirely!
